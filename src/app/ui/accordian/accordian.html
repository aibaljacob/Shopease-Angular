Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before placing your order.

We accept all major credit and debit cards, UPI, Net Banking, digital wallets, and Cash on Delivery (available in selected locations). All transactions are protected using secure payment gateways.

Standard delivery usually takes 3–7 business days, while express delivery is available in selected cities and typically arrives within 1–2 business days. You'll receive tracking details once your order is shipped.

Most products can be returned within 7 days of delivery if they're unused and in their original packaging. Once your return is approved, the refund will be processed to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.

Orders can be cancelled or modified before they are shipped. Visit My Orders in your account to request a cancellation. Once the order has been dispatched, you can use our return policy after delivery.
